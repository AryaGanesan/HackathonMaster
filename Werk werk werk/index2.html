What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event that anyone who has an interest in technology can attend. Participants at the hackathon learn, build, and share their creations over a period of time in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. (mlh.io) Our hackathon is the first hackathon in Loudoun County Public Schools. This event is 12 hours long and consists of workshops and competitions.
