About the Hackathon

First off, what is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event that anyone who has an interest in technology can attend. Participants at the hackathon learn, build, and share their creations over a period of time in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere (mlh.io).

Imagine, build, but most importantly, have fun!

Our hackathon is the first hackathon in Loudoun County Public Schools, so we're hoping to make it grand-slam! Join now for a wild day of fun experiences. This event is 12 hours long and consists of workshops and competitions.

FAQ

  • What is Hack Academies?

    Hack Academies is a student-run hackathon at the Academies of Loudoun, where you will have 12 hours to learn how to code and/or work with your friends to make your idea come to life.

  • When is Hack Academies?

    Hack Academies will be on May 18th . Registration closes at 1pm on Saturday. No entry after this time.

  • Who can attend?

    Hack Academies is open to any and all current high school students. If you're not a high school student but would like to attend, consider coming as a mentor or volunteer.

  • Is there any cost?

    Nope! Thanks to our sponsors, Hack Academies is completely free!

  • Is there a deadline?

    Registration will be open starting on April 5th through April 26th.

  • Do you have a code of conduct?

    Yes, good question! We have adopted the MLH code of conduct, which all attendees will be required to adhere to.

  • Isn't hacking illegal?

    In the context of a hackathon, "hacking" is to build a real application over the length of the event. It's important to not confuse this with illegal hacking, which is gaining unauthorized access to a computer.

  • Do I need a team?

    If you already have a team of 2-4 people you want to work with, that's awesome! If not don't worry because we'll have an opportunity for people to join a team if they need one.

  • What should I bring?

    You'll need to bring your laptop, phone, and chargers. We suggest you bring a set of nice clothes for demos; you'll want to make the best impression.

  • Will there be food?

    Yes. Lots and lots of food.

If your question is not answered here, just ask us: academies.hackathon@gmail.com.
